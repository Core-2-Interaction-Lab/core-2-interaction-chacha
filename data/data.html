Wild Encounters
Revealing the Hidden World of Central Park's Furry Residents
The Squirrel Census has collected squirrel data on a total of 3,023 sightings in Central Park. The data provides an overview of the population of squirrels in the park, including their distribution and behavior patterns. The dataset includes information on the squirrel's behavior, including foraging, eating, climbing, chasing, and running.
